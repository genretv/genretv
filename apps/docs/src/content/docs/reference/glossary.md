---
title: Glossary
description: GenreTV terms used throughout the app and help pages.
---

**Canonical List**

The system-owned default schedule shown to signed-out visitors and used as every Personal List's baseline.

**Personal List**

The resolved list a signed-in user sees after applying their own additions, overrides, hidden items, and imports to the Canonical List.

**List Overlay**

The user-owned changes that distinguish a Personal List from its canonical baseline.

**Show**

A television series or one-off title. Shows are the primary management unit and own Seasons.

**Season**

A numbered or release-oriented part of a Show. A Season can also represent a special, movie, pilot, or other Extra Release.

**Episode**

An optional individual installment within a Season. A Season remains valid with no Episode rows.

**Release Window**

An exact date or an imprecise month, meteorological season, or year describing expected release timing.

**Schedule Section**

A date-sensitive grouping: Now Showing, Upcoming, Awaiting Renewal or Cancellation, or Finished.

**Historical Season**

A known Season retained in management but omitted from the Schedule because a later Season represents the Show's current state.

**Linked import**

A Season import that continues receiving source changes for fields the recipient has not overridden.

**Detached copy**

An imported Season that becomes independent from future source changes.

**Published snapshot**

A versioned public capture of a publisher's resolved list at the time they publish it.

**Canonical Proposal**

A request for a maintainer to review and selectively merge a personal Show, Season, or Episode contribution into the Canonical List.

**Unknown value**

Deliberately missing information. Unknown metadata is valid and is not replaced with invented placeholder values.
