---
title: Canonical proposals
description: Send your Show, Season, or Episode changes to canonical maintainers.
---

**Requires:** current publishing permission.

Editing your Personal List never directly changes the Canonical List. When a personal Show, Season, or Episode would improve the shared data, open its management editor and use **Send to canonical**.

Add a note that helps a maintainer understand the source, correction, or reason for inclusion. The proposal captures the relevant item and required parent context so maintainers can review it without manually reconstructing your hierarchy.

## What happens next

The proposal appears in the maintainer review queue and creates an internal notification. A maintainer can:

- **Approve + merge** selected proposed fields and records;
- **Reject** a proposal that should not be incorporated; or
- **Close** a duplicate or otherwise completed request without merging it.

Approval is field-selective. Existing canonical Shows, Seasons, or Episodes can be updated, while missing parent or child records can be created where needed. A proposal does not grant you direct canonical editing permission.
