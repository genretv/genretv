title: Welcome to GenreTV
description: Learn what GenreTV tracks and what signing in adds.
---

GenreTV tracks science-fiction, fantasy, supernatural, superhero, and related television releases. Anyone can browse the shared **Canonical List**. Signing in adds a **Personal List** that starts with the same canonical schedule and records only your own changes.

## Browse without an account

Open [the Schedule](/) to:

- switch among Now Showing, Upcoming, Awaiting Renewal or Cancellation, and Finished;
- search and filter the active section;
- sort its table and choose 20, 50, or 100 rows per page;
- open a row's extra details; and
- follow available IMDb and official platform links.

Your schedule filters and ordering are saved in this browser. They are viewing preferences, not changes to the Canonical List.

## What signing in adds

With an account you can:

- manage your own Shows, Seasons, and optional Episodes;
- hide inherited rows from your Personal List;
- import linked or independent copies from published lists;
- create a public profile;
- apply for permission to publish snapshots; and
- export your resolved Personal List or complete local database.

New accounts do not copy every canonical row. Your empty overlay resolves to the Canonical List until you make a personal change.

## Create an account

Select **Sign in**, then **Create account**. Enter an email address and password. If email confirmation is enabled, follow the confirmation message before signing in.

GenreTV creates and prepares a local browser database during initial loading. After sign-in, that database is associated with your account and user-specific data begins synchronizing.
