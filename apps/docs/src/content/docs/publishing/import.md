---
title: Import from published lists
description: Choose between linked imports and detached copies.
---

Anyone can browse [Published Lists](/#/published). **Importing requires a signed-in account.**

Imports are Season-centered and include the parent Show. A numbered Season also brings prerequisite known numbered Seasons needed for a coherent history, plus known Episodes where available.

## Linked import

A **Linked import** is the default. It continues to receive source changes for fields you have not overridden. Use it when you trust the publisher and want later corrections without repeatedly importing the same Season.

The published-list table marks Seasons already linked into your Personal List. You can remove a linked import from that table. Removing it removes the link from your list; it does not alter the publisher's snapshot.

## Detached copy

A **Copy** becomes independent after import. It may retain provenance, but future source changes do not flow into it. Choose this when you intend to diverge from the published metadata.

## Already imported

GenreTV prevents duplicate import actions for a Season already present in the corresponding mode and shows its imported state. Manage the resulting Show and Season from [Manage](/#/manage).
