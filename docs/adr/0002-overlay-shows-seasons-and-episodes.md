# Overlay Shows, Seasons, and Episodes

Personal Lists will store overlay changes at the Show, Season, and Episode levels, while MVP imports from public lists happen as Season Copies. Every persisted Show has one or more Season rows, and importing a numbered Season also carries or creates the prerequisite numbered Seasons implied by it. Episodes remain optional and are copied only when known. This keeps full list history available to management while the public Schedule View projects only schedule-relevant Seasons.
